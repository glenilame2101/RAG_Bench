"Frank Thorpe (public servant)"
Frank Thorpe (public servant) Frank Gordon Thorpe, (15 February 1885 – 30 March 1967) was a senior Australian public servant. He was Public Service Commissioner between 1936 and 1947. Frank Thorpe was born on 15 February 1885 in Kooreh, Victoria. Thorpe started his Commonwealth Public Service career in 1901, one of the Australia's earliest Commonwealth public servants, appointed as a personal clerk to the deputy Postmaster General in the Postmaster-General's Department.
