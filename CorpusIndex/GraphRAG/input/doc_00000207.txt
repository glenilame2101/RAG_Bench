Georgia Tech became a charter member of the Metro Conference in 1975 (the first season started in 1976), and then became the eighth member of the ACC in 1978 (starting play in 1979). As of the 2007–08 season, the Yellow Jackets have won three ACC Tournament championships and been the ACC's top seed twice.
