Albert Einstein was a theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity. He was born in Ulm, Germany in 1879. Einstein worked at Princeton University in New Jersey. He won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921. His famous equation is E=mc^2.

Einstein collaborated with other scientists including Niels Bohr and Max Planck. He befriended Charlie Chaplin, who invited Einstein to the premiere of his film City Lights.
